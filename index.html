Objectives

These are the objectives of the oil and gas due diligence team

  • Development of oil and gas producing communities

    to promote the development of oil and gas producing communities through the provisions of portable drinking water, primary health care facilities, social amenities and scholarship for indigent children and youths.

  • To liaise with government agencies

    To liaise with government agencies, local authorities and oil and gas companies and major stakeholders in the development of oil and gas producing communities.

  • surveillance

    To carry out surveillance on the activities of oil exploration and exploitation companies in order to ward off environmental pollution and gas flaring.

  • Prevent Pollution

    To prevent environmental pollution especially destruction of the ecosystem by negligent activities of oil and gas companies and multi - nationals in oil and gas producing communities in Nigeria.

  • Promote Peace

    To promote peaceful co-existance between host communities and oil and gas companies carrying out legitimate activities

  • Promoting Accountability

    To promote transparency and accountability in the oil and gas industry by demanding for publication of its activities, operation of licenses and community development by relevant government agencies and authorities

  • Activity

    To organize workshops, seminars for community leaders in oil and gas producing communities on the need for protecting installations and equipment in the oil and gas industry within their domain
